<div className="mt-16 mb-16 max-w-5xl mx-auto px-6">
  <h2 className="text-3xl font-bold dark:text-white text-gray-900 mb-4">Quick Start</h2>
  <p className="text-base dark:text-gray-400 text-gray-600 mb-8">
    Get up and running with Reforestation Saga in just a few steps
  </p>

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Launch the Game">
      Open Reforestation Saga on your iOS device. You'll be greeted with the home screen showing your highest level achieved.
      
      <Note>
        The game requires camera access for eye-blink detection. Make sure to grant camera permissions when prompted.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Complete Identity Verification">
      Tap **Tap to Play** to begin your mission. You'll need to verify your identity by aligning your face with the camera scanner. The system uses the Vision framework to detect your face and eyes.
      
      ```swift
      // The game detects blinks using Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR)
      if leftEAR < 0.1 && rightEAR < 0.1 {
          if !self.isEyeClosed {
              self.isEyeClosed = true
          }
      }
      ```
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Start Your Mission">
      After verification, you'll receive your mission briefing. Tap **Start Mission** to begin planting trees. Each blink shoots a tree seedling toward the rotating planet.
      
      <Tip>
        Wait for the initial blink alert to disappear before starting. Your first blink will plant your first tree!
      </Tip>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Plant Trees and Progress">
      Blink to plant trees on the green areas of the rotating planet. Avoid the ocean (blue areas) and existing trees. Complete each level by planting all required trees.
      
      <Accordion title="Level requirements">
        - Level 1-5: 3-7 trees needed, 4-second rotation
        - Level 6-10: 8-12 trees needed, 3-second rotation, random direction
        - Level 11+: 13+ trees needed, 1.5-4 second rotation, random direction
      </Accordion>
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
